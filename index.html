Questions, answered.

Is ObaPrep only on iPhone? +

For now, yes. ObaPrep is built for iOS first. If you're on Android, join the waitlist and we'll tell you the moment there's a version for you.

When does it launch? +

Soon. It's submitted to the App Store and going through Apple's review. Join the waitlist and you'll hear from us the day it's live.

How is this different from Anki, Quizlet, or Magoosh? +

ObaPrep is GRE vocabulary in one place, paced to your test date. Real GRE-style questions are built in, the scheduling is automatic, and there's nothing to set up: no decks to build, no settings to tune.

What do I get for free? +

Your whole first session: a quick demo, a short diagnostic, your study plan, and the first round of words. After that, Pro unlocks unlimited sessions with a 14-day free trial.

Do I need internet to study? +

No. Your words and your progress live on your phone, so you can study on a plane or underground.
